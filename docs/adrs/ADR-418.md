# ADR-418: KVKK Compliance Retention Policy Schedule (Revision 88)

## Status
Accepted

## Context
During high-concurrency Turkish call center audio ingestion and speech recognition processing, the system must ensure deterministic latency, high transcription accuracy, and strict KVKK data privacy compliance.

## Decision
We adopt the KVKK Compliance Retention Policy Schedule strategy to enforce automated 90-day purging of unredacted raw audio recordings pursuant to KVKK Article 7.

## Rationale
- Improves end-to-end audio processing throughput and acoustic pipeline stability.
- Ensures compatibility with Turkish telephony 8kHz PCM G.711 and 16kHz HD voice codecs.
- Aligns with enterprise audit trail requirements and speaker diarization accuracy benchmarks.
