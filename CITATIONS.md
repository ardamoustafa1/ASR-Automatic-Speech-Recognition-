# 📚 Citations & Acknowledgements

ASR-Pro stands on the shoulders of giants. We utilize several state-of-the-art open-source machine learning models to power our intelligence engines. If you use ASR-Pro in your academic research, please consider citing the following papers and repositories:

## 1. Whisper (OpenAI)
We utilize `faster-whisper` which is a CTranslate2 reimplementation of OpenAI's Whisper model.
```bibtex
@article{radford2022robust,
  title={Robust Speech Recognition via Large-Scale Weak Supervision},
  author={Radford, Alec and Kim, Jong Wook and Xu, Tao and Brockman, Greg and McLeavey, Christine and Sutskever, Ilya},
  journal={arXiv preprint arXiv:2212.04356},
  year={2022}
}
```

## 2. Faster-Whisper (Guillaume Klein & Systran)
